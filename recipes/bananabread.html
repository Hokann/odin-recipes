Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a fairly simple and easy to make. It's also crazy good. Because of that, it's become quite a famous type of bread to make. On top of bananas, nuts, chocolate chips and fruit like blueberries can be
added to the bread for added flavor and texture. It is also possible to make Gluten-free banana bread, although this recipe will not cover it. See this Gluten-free banana bread recipe for more info.

This recipe provides around 10 servings from 1 loaf.
From start to finish, it should take around an hour to prepare the bread.
The ingredients are fairly inexpensive and easy to obtain, it should cost you around 3$ to make a loaf, unless maybe you shop at Whole Foods.
