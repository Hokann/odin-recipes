Chicken Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a cooked tortilla (usually corn) filled with mainly cheese, but meat, cooked vegetables and spices can be added
aswell. In this recipe, we'll be making a chicken quesadilla with some cooked veggies. This dish is extremely simple, cheap, and
fast to make. Perfect for students! The quesadilla is a famous mexican dish, that is served across the world.

Many variations of the original quesadilla have been created by using different ingredients such as goat cheese, or beans. More
outlandish variations have also surfaced, such as the pizzadilla, the breakfast quesadilla or even the dessert quesadilla.
